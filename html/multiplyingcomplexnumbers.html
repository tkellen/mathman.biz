The angles are 14°= tan-1 (b/a) = tan-1(1/4), and 56°= tan-1(3/2) and 14° + 56° = 70°. So (4 + i) * (2 + 3 i) is a complex number whose length is   and angle is 70°. Using the distributive law (4 + i) * (2 + 3 i) = 4* (2 + 3 i) +  i * (2 + 3 i) =  8 + 12 i +  -3 + 2 i  =  5 + 14 i.

On the graph 4* (2 + 3 i) has length 4 times (2 + 3 i), and i * (2 + 3 i) takes (2 + 3 i) and rotates it 90° counter-clockwise. Then you add these. The length of 5 + 14 i  =   and its angle is 70° (= tan-1(14/5)).
