The Shuttle Puzzle or Peg Game

The object of the game is to interchange the red pegs with the black pegs

Rules of the game: 1.) You can move a peg to an open space next to one, or  2.) you can jump, but only one peg of the other color, and 3.) you can't move backwards.

                                 

The Math:

A.) In a table, keep track of the # of pairs of pegs (x) vs the # of moves to interchange them (y)

B.) Graph the pairs of numbers in the table

 C.) Find a rule to get from x to y
