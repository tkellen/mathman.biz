The Gregory-Leibnitz series:

It turns out that the limit of this series

  is a slowly converging series.

A program in basic which approximates p using this series is at the right.

 

10 x=0

20 b=0

30 a=(-1)^x *(1/(2 *x+1))

40 c=a+b

50 PRINT 4*c

60 b=c

70 x=x+1

80 IF x>20 THEN STOP

GOTO 30
