2c. Graph 3 successive 'pieces' of the infinite continued fraction.
Notice that these three functions, which are each one piece of an hyperbola, intersect at (2,2) and (3,3), the two roots of the quadratic equation x2 - 5x + 6 = 0 . WOW!
