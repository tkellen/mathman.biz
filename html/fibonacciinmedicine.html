How Fibonacci Numbers are used in Changing the Dosage of a Medicine

The example below will do the case of decreasing the dosage of a medicine, in 5 steps, say.

We'll look at the Fibonacci numbers that decrease by 5 ...that would be from 13 to 8..

The first dosage would be 8/13 * (the original dosage). The next dosage below that would be 5/8 * (the original dosage), then 3/5, then 2/3, then 1/2 of the original dose would be the last dosage.
