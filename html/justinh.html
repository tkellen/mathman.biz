One comment about writing down the numbers for say, the square root of 2 (see p. A211)..I would recommend writing down the guesses like this (getting the numbers to the nearest tenth, hundredth, thousandth...) narrowing down the answer.

Too big ->     2,   1.5,   1.42,  1.415 , ...

Too small -> 1,   1.4,   1.41,  1.414 , ...

then one can see that you are getting 2 infinite sequences, each approaches the square root of 2, one from below, the other from above. You can even square the number to see if you get 2.
