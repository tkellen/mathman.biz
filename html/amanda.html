Amanda, a 7th grader, does some mathematics

1. 8/22/01 While graphing the pairs of numbers for the Shuttle Puzzle- (1,3), (2, 8) and (3, 15), Amanda realized these points did not form a straight line! From her experiences, she thought all graphs were straight lines. As she continued plotting the points (0,0) and (-1, -1) and (- 2, 0), she realized it was going back up; Don told her its name is a parabola, a very important curve in nature and mathematics. The axis of symmetry is x = -1, and the vertex, the lowest or highest point, is (-1, -1). Don showed her the pattern in going up from (0,0)- right 1, up 1, right 1, up 3, right 1, up 5, and so on- it goes up the odd numbers.
