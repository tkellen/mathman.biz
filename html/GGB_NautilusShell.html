The Nautilus Shell applet

This applet shows the curve, which is the graph of the equation of the Nautilus Shell. Move P
around the curve. What do you see?

Notice some things about the graph:
What happens to angle β as P is moved around the curve? β remains constant, which makes th Nautilus Shell an Equiangular Spiral.

What is the ratio of the radii (larger to smaller) of the curve as P is rotated from 0 to 2Pi? This gives the growth rate of the Nautilus Shell, about 3.2 and also is a constant every 2Pi radians.
